Backpack Buddies Foundation of Loudoun

So Who are These People Anyway?

Backpack Buddies Foundation of Loudoun is a charitable organization based in Loudoun County, dedicated to serving the local area children. They partner with the local
school system to give weekend, summer, and holiday meals to those kids who would be otherwise unable to have food at those times. They have been around since March of 2014, making them about 3 years old.

How to help

There are many ways you can help their cause. For one, they are always looking for volunteers. To
sign up, just click here to go to the Backpack Buddies of

About Food Loudoun

What is our Mission?

We bring about change in Loudoun County, Virginia. Thousands, 14,170 to be precise, are living with
food insecurity. Food insecurity, as defined by the US Department of Agriculture, is lack of
access, at times, to adequate foods required to live an active life. Those people do not always
have food insecurity, but when they are trying to pay off medical bills, dealing with an addiction,
or even out of a job, they can run into food insecurity and need someone to help them.

Who runs this blog?

Well, I am AJ Bushman. I am an 8th grade student at Trailside Middle School in Loudoun County. I
felt that this was such a major thing to write about because I realized that there might be students
at my school that do not have adequate access to food and not many people know their struggle.
