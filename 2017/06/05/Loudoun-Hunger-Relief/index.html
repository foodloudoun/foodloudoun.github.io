Loudoun Hunger Relief

Who are these people

Loudoun Hunger Relief is a non-profit, formerly known as Loudoun Interfaith Relief. They offer
services to those without food in Loudoun County. They have been around since 1991, when the local
churches decided to come together and create a united food bank, rather than have individual,
smaller food closets. They let people schedule appointments to come in and get enough food for 3
days, which, for some kids with food insecurity, is what they need. And plus, those three meals are
carefully planned to be healthy, not just a hodge-podge of food put together.
